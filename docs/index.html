Why having the articles also as a separate website?

For a couple of reasons:

  1. For convenience: it might be simpler for some to read them in such a format than on LinkedIn.
  2. For readability: these are here written in markdown and this allows for additional capability - especially when it comes to code.
  3. For contributions: as all the website is hosted on GitHub, experts can contribute to improve the content. Sometimes a few added words can clarify a sentence or some small additions can lead to great insights. I am open to your suggestions!
